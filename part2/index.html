From now on, you will be listening to normal binaural recordings of different environmental background sounds instead of recordings from a dummy head wearing headphones. These recording will represent sounds that you hear when you're wearing a noise-cancelling headphones but this function is turned off. These will be the reference and assigned a value of 0. You will be asked to evaluate different modified versions of this reference based on a scale from 0 to 10. Remember to evaluate your experience at and after the transition.

•   5:  "I clearly feel this pressurised feeling in my ear".

•   10:  "not only I experience this effect clearly, it makes me feel so uncomfortable, that I want to swallow like in an airplane to ease this feeling."

•   Anything belows 5:  "I feel something, but I'm not sure if it is indeed eardrum suck effect."

Remember if you feel tired, take a break, take the headphones off, and whenever you're ready, you can click start below. But during your break, you shouldn't close the browser or your evaluation won't be recorded on the server.
