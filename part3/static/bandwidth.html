What to evaluate?

Evaluate the pressurised feeling in your ear (e.g like when you are in an ascending/descending airplane) on a scale from 0-10 when you are listening to the samples. Pressurised here can also be described informally as feeling your ear being blocked, clogged or muffled

  • 0: you feel nothing.
  • below 5: You feel something, but you are not sure if it is the sensation being described.
  • 5: You feel this sensation clearly.
  • 10: The pressurised feeling is very severe and uncomfortable, I feel like I need to swallow like in an ascending airplane.

How to interact with the interface?

  • Use the number keys on your keyboard (0 - maximum number of samples) to select a sample.
  • key number 0 corresponds to the reference sample, which always have an evaluation of 0.
  • After you've chosen a sample, the corresponding audio will be played and looped (replayed automatically after it ends), the corresponding label will changed color so you can keep track of what you are listening to.
  • You can only control the slider corresponding to the audio you are listening to.
  • You can only use the arrow up and arrow down key on your keyboard to move the slider.

How to compare between samples?

Supposed you've listened to Sample #1 and your first impression is to give it an evaluation of 7. Then you listen to sample #2 and you feel that the effect in sample #2 is way more obvious and maybe that should be a 7, you should then switch back and fourth between 1 and 2 to assign each an appropriate value. Repeat this process to each new samples that you hear to get a more correct impression instead of relying solely on your first impression.
