To help you better understand this phenomenon, in this part you will be listening to actual recordings of this effect, recorded using a dummy head wearing an Active-Noise-Cancelling headphones in our lab, which mimics our actual head with microphones placed where our eardrum supposed to be. This means that what you hear is actually what the dummy head heard in our lab 😁. This recording will be our baseline reference with an assigned evaluation of 0, you will be asked to evaluate different modification of this recording on a scale from -5 (the effect got significantly amplified) to 5 (the effect completely disappears). Remember to evaluate your experience at and after the transition.
