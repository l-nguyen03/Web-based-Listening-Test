What you should make sure during the tests?

  • You should do these tests in a quiet room with minimal surrounding sounds.
  • Use over-ear headphones or in-ear headphones that has a good seal in your ear canal to ensure a maximum blockage of external factors.
  • If your headphones have Noise-Cancellation and/or Transparency Mode, make sure to turn them all off before starting.
  • The audio samples are normalised so you should be able to listen to the sound at 80% to 100%. The higher the volume the better.
  • Don't go below 80% volume or some features of the samples will be lost.
  • At any point during this evaluation you feel tired, you can take off the headphones, rest and comback later.
  • !!! Only refresh the browser during the test if the sounds doesn't come out when you press the keys !!!
  • !!! Don't close the browser until you reach the end of the test !!!

Test Series Structure

There are 5 parts with the following structures:

  • Each part consists of 5 tests.
  • Each part investigates a potential factor that can lead to this effect.
  • Each test represents a different listening environment.
