What is it about?

  • "Eardrum suck effect": This is the sensation that many users of Active-Noise-Cancelling (abr. ANC) Headphones have. It feels like your ear is somehow pressurised, or informally, blocked, muffled or clogged or even sucked out.
    • For example: A similar sensation is when you are in a ascending/descending airplane or in a high speed elevator and your ear is somehow blocked and you need to swallow to listen normally again.
  • You will be given several audio samples and asked to rate your listening sensation with a given scale (which will be described before the corresponding test), e.g. from 0 to 10
